# 🎵 Focus Music Feature - Usage Guide

## Overview
The Focus Music feature allows you to play music while working to help you focus better. You can use royalty-free presets or upload your own music files for the best experience.

## Quick Start

1. ✅ Click the blue music icon (bottom-right corner)
2. ✅ Choose a song or upload your own
3. ✅ Click the play icon
4. ✅ Adjust volume with the slider

## Features

### 1. **Upload Your Own Music (Recommended)**
For best results, **upload your own music files**:
- Click the "Upload Music" button
- Select any audio file (MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, etc.)
- Maximum file size: 20MB
- Your uploaded music is saved locally in your browser
- **Uploaded music plays instantly without delays!**

### 2. **Royalty-Free Music Presets**
Pre-loaded slow music tracks available:
- Ambient Calm
- Focus Flow
- Deep Concentration
- Peaceful Vibes

**Note:** Presets may have loading delays due to external CDN connectivity. For seamless playback, consider uploading your own music instead.

### 3. **Volume Control**
- Use the slider in the Music Menu to adjust volume
- Volume setting is automatically saved

### 4. **Music Playback**
- Click play icon or music title to start playing
- Music loops continuously
- Currently playing track is highlighted in blue
- **Browser requires user click to start playing** (this is a browser security feature)

## How to Use

### Step 1: Open Music Menu
- Click the blue music icon floating in the bottom-right corner

### Step 2: Play Music
- **Option A - Upload Your Own:** Click "Upload Music" to select a file from your device
- **Option B - Use Presets:** Click on any preset music (may have loading time)

### Step 3: Start Playing
- Click the play icon next to the song
- Music will start playing after you click

### Step 4: Adjust Volume
- Drag the volume slider at the top of the menu
- Volume changes apply immediately

### Step 5: Manage Your Music
- To pause: Click the pause icon
- To delete uploaded music: Click the trash icon
- To switch songs: Just click a different song

## Technical Details

### Local Storage
- ✅ Uploaded music uses Blob URLs (stored locally in browser)
- ✅ Settings are saved to localStorage
- ✅ No files uploaded to servers
- ⚠️ Clearing browser cache will delete uploaded music files

### Browser Compatibility
- Works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Requires user interaction (click) to start playing
- Supports all common audio formats (MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, etc.)

### Presets
- Uses external CDN for streaming
- May be affected by network connectivity
- Consider uploading your own for reliability

## Troubleshooting

### Music Won't Play
1. ✅ Make sure you clicked on the song (browser requires user interaction)
2. ✅ Check your system volume isn't muted
3. ✅ Try uploading your own music file instead

### Upload Failed
1. ✅ Check file size is less than 20MB
2. ✅ Verify it's an audio file (not video)
3. ✅ Try a different file format
4. ✅ Refresh the page and try again

### Preset Music Has Delays
1. ✅ This is normal (external CDN loading)
2. ✅ **Recommendation:** Upload your own music instead for instant playback
3. ✅ Check your internet connection

### Music Disappeared After Browser Restart
1. ✅ If you cleared browser cache, uploaded music files are deleted
2. ✅ Re-upload your music files
3. ✅ Consider backing up your music files separately
